How to run these activities
👥
Split the class into teams of 3–4. Display this page on the projector and let a student click through.
📋
Each activity starts with a whiteboard prompt — write it up before you begin so teams know what to map out.
💬
Give teams 5 minutes to discuss and write their answers on the whiteboard before revealing the answer.
🏆
Award a point to the team with the best reasoning — not just the right answer, but the best justification.
